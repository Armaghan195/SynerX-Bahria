About SynerX

Where Academia
Meets Industry

SynerX 2026 is Bahria University's premier student-centric flagship experience positioned at the intersection of technology, entrepreneurship, athletics, and expressive arts. More than an event, it is a high-impact platform that translates classroom knowledge into industry-aligned execution.

Multi-Domain Synergy

Advanced technology, competitive sports, and cultural expression coexist on a single national platform one arena, every dimension.

Curated Professionalism

Student-led, professionally governed every segment evaluated by industry experts and seasoned practitioners against real-world standards.

National Legacy

A legacy platform that attracts the top 2% of student talent from universities across Pakistan a mark of distinction that stands beyond the event.

Event Verticals

Compete. Perform. Innovate.

Eight distinct arenas of competition and expression each designed to challenge your limits and showcase your strengths on a national stage.

Innovation & Tech

Push the boundaries of technology in high-pressure engineering challenges, coding marathons, and innovation sprints judged by real-world tech professionals.

Hackathon Speed Programming Robotics Innovation Lab

Sports Arena

Elite athletic competition across multiple disciplines. Represent your university, compete against the nation's best, and claim your place at the top.

Futsal Table Tennis Arm Wrestling Volleyball

Culture & Arts

A celebration of creativity, heritage, and expression. From electrifying stage performances to intimate poetry nights the stage belongs to every voice.

Battle of Bands Stage Play Comedy Night Qawali Night Open Mic

Esports

The digital arena opens. Compete in high-stakes gaming tournaments with a live crowd, a dedicated stage, and a competitive bracket that separates the best from the rest.

Gaming Tournaments Esports Finals Live Stage

Entrepreneurship

Pitch your vision to a panel of investors and industry leaders. From concept to the main stage in days build, refine, and present your next big idea under real pressure.

Venture Pitch Industry Judges Live Panel

Model United Nations

A four-day immersive diplomatic simulation. Debate global issues, forge bloc alliances, draft resolutions, and sharpen the oratory skills that define tomorrow's leaders.

4-Day Conference Multiple Committees Best Delegate

Auto & Bike Show

An exclusive automotive showcase where machines meet culture. Custom builds, performance vehicles, and rare exhibits across two dedicated show days a first for a university event of this scale.

Auto Show Bike Show Custom Builds

Showcase groundbreaking student projects and scientific experiments. A platform for innovators to present original work to peers, faculty, and industry observers.

Project Expo Science Olympiad Innovation Displays
